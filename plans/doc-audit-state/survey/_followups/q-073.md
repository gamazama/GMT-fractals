## Question
> `helpUtils.collectHelpIds`, `types/help.ContextMenuItem`, `openContextMenu` (used by AudioPanel.tsx:7, 238–245 and AudioSpectrum.tsx:7, 14, 383–392) — help/context-menu plumbing outside scope.

## Answer
The three symbols are the right-click/help plumbing shared across the app:

- `collectHelpIds(target)` at `utils/helpUtils.ts:2-22` walks up the DOM from an event target, gathering `data-help-id` attributes (space-separated) into an ordered list (most-specific first). Used to populate the help-menu context for a clicked element.
- `ContextMenuItem` at `types/help.ts:12-34` is the shape consumed by `GlobalContextMenu` — label/action plus optional icon, danger/checked/header/disabled/keepOpen flags, nested `children`, and two extension modes (`type: 'slider'` legacy snapshot, or live `element: ReactNode`).
- `openContextMenu(x, y, items, targetHelpId)` is one of the callbacks on `StoreCallbacksContext` (see `components/contexts/StoreCallbacksContext.tsx`); panels call it to pop the global menu.

So `openContextMenu` is already owned by e13-shared-ui (which also owns `GlobalContextMenu` and `StoreCallbacksContext`); the help-id collection util and the `types/help.ts` types are orphans that logically belong with e13's context-menu/help plumbing.
